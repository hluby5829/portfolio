About me

I am an engineer first and designer a distant second. That being said, this was my first foray into the unknown world of UIUX and gave it my best shot.

Working on these projects has allowed me to create my own personal style. I knew what types of designs I liked -- light, minimal, etc. -- but was unsure how to actually acheive this style in a way that was accessible to all. It was only after fiddling with things by myself that I was able to land on the style that best represents me.

This portfolio contains some of the work I accomplished over the past couple of months in CS1300. They include an account of my trials and tribulations as I tried my best to balance the utility and aesthetics necessary to produce good designs.

I hope you enjoy.
